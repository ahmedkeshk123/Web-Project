Core Functionality To Our Gold Mine
Exploration And Evaluation

  1. The work begins by conducting exploratory studies to determine the presence of gold in the area
  2. This includes the use of techniques such as geological and geophysical prospecting

Planning And Design

  1. The operational structure of the mine is determined and the necessary infrastructure is designed.
  2. This includes the design of excavation and material transportation systems.

Prospecting

  1. The process of prospecting is done to find the layers that contain gold.
  2. Heavy equipment such as excavators and bulldozers are used to move rocks and soil.

Granulation And Grinding

  1. The rocks are extracted and converted into small particles in granulation and grinding plants.
  2. Crushers and mills are often used for this purpose.

Gold Extraction

  1. Gold is separated from rocks using chemicals such as cyanide.
  2. Gold reacts with chemicals to be extracted

Gold Refining

  1. After gold is extracted, it is transported to refineries for purification.
  2. This includes processes such as heating and melting to eliminate impurities.

Export

After refining, the gold is exported to be converted into finished products.

Follow-up And Monitoring

The mine is monitored periodically to ensure its compliance with safety and environmental standards
